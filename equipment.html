Volleyball Essentials

Gameplay

There is not much equipment needed to get a game of volleyball going. The main equipment is:
  1. A volleyball

  2. A net

  3. A court

What To Wear For Volleyball

Players in competitive volleyball like to gear up to get the best experience. This is what they wear:
  1. Knee Pads

  2. Arm Sleeves

  3. Proper Footwear- Volleyball Shoes

  4. Volleyball shorts

  5. Volleyball shirts
